info

 
Since graduating from Nova Scotia Community College’s Recording Arts program in 2004, Adam Gallant has amassed ten years of recording and production experience. Currently, Adam owns and operates a The Hill Sound Studio Inc. in the core of downtown Charlottetown.
 
Album credits range from Recording Engineer, Mastering Engineer, Producer, Mixing Engineer, to Musician.
 
Video credits include Location Audio, Post Audio, Sound Design and Composition.
 
Adam currently plays guitar and records with Racoon Bandit.
